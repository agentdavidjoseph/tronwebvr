TRON WebVR

An quick example of WebVR. Make sure you have download a WebVR enabled browser such as Firefox Nightly or Brandon Jones' Chromium Builds.

Press 'F' to enter "The Grid".

I'll be speaking about this demo and other ways to get started in VR at EmpireJS.
